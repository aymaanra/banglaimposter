🕵️ Bangla Imposter

Everyone secretly sees the same secret word — except the imposter, who only gets a hint. Say related words, sniff them out!

4

2 imposters need at least 6 players.

How to play
  1. Pass the phone around. Each player taps once to secretly see the word.
  2. The imposter sees only a hint — they must bluff.
  3. Going around the circle, everyone says one word related to the secret word.
  4. Not sure who's faking? Play another round.
  5. Accuse out loud, then reveal the imposter!
